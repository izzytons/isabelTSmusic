Isabel Tonelli-Sippel

Proudly endorsed by InEarz Audio, Izzy is an accomplished guitarist currently performing with Dynamite Rhythm, Rave On, and Steve Daggett. Having worked in numerous pit bands and other ensembles around New England, she has performed at venues such as Mechanics Hall, Boston Symphony Hall, the Woodstock Fair, The Big E Court of Honor Stage, and the Newport Jazz Festival. Izzy recently graduated from Clark University with a Master's in Biology and is now working as a software engineer for the TJX Companies.
