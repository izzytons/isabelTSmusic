Isabel Tonelli-Sippel

Proudly endorsed by InEarz Audio, Izzy is an accomplished guitarist currently performing with Dynamite Rhythm, and Rave On. Having worked in numerous pit bands and other ensembles around New England, she has performed at venues such as Mechanics Hall, Boston Symphony Hall, the Woodstock Fair, The Big E Court of Honor Stage, and the Newport Jazz Festival. Izzy is currently pursuing a Biology Masters degree at Clark University.
